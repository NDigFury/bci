Introduction

For many years the long standing of BCI goes into two categories:

  • Non-invasive or EEG
  • Surgical installed adapter and reciever

    • These two features listen to the brain and do not have a impluse to return information. The brain interaction only sends the information to the device and not the other way around. I am currently a research assistant for the multimedia augmented reailty and virtual reality lab. This topic is what I am doing my research paper on.

    Various Companies

    Wearable EEG just read the impluses that are detectible on the surface of the scalp. Currently there are various companies out there attempting to progress the science.

    Neuralink

    a company owned by Elon Musk, creates medical applications via bluetooth to send a signal from the brain to different devices like a computer, phone, keyboard, or actuator. This company currently threads into the brain attempting to help those with memory loss, hearing loss, blindness, and other medical conditions.
